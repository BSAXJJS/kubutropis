    WELCOME TO

    KUBU TROPIS

    Kubu Tropis provides rooms with free WiFi in the Ubud City-Centre of Ubud, less than 1 km from Ubud Palace. The property is located a 12-minute walk from Ubud Market, 1.1 km from The Blanco Renaissance Museum and 2.6 km from Neka Art Museum. Ubud Monkey Forest is 2.7 km from the property.

    At the guesthouse, rooms come with a desk. Each room has air conditioning, and some rooms also feature a terrace. At Kubu Tropis every room comes with a seating area.

    Elephant Cave is 5.8 km from the accommodation. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 38.6 km from the property.
